⌄ DISCOVER THE PROJECT
01

NOT ALL TRIBUTE BANDS ARE BORN THE SAME WAY.

Some choose simply to perform the songs again.

Some focus on looking like the artist.

ETB was born from a different idea.

To bring back to the audience the emotions, atmosphere and musical richness of Eros Ramazzotti’s greatest concerts, with the utmost respect for the work behind every live show.

⌄ CONTINUE
02

IT IS NOT JUST ABOUT THE SONGS.

Everyone knows the songs.

The real challenge is recreating what happens when those songs come alive on stage.

The dynamics.
The arrangements.
The backing vocals.
The nuances.
The silences.
The energy.

Every element helps build an experience that goes far beyond a simple musical performance.

⌄ CONTINUE
03

BEHIND EVERY LIVE SHOW THERE IS MUCH MORE THAN THE AUDIENCE SEES.

Every ETB concert is the result of work that begins long before the first note.

Arrangement study.
Sound research.
Programming.
Equipment checks.
Connection checks.
Technical organisation.

Every detail is prepared to minimise the unexpected and allow the band to focus entirely on what truly matters: the music.

⌄ CONTINUE
04

RELIABILITY CANNOT BE IMPROVISED.

A successful show does not depend on luck.

It comes from preparation.
Experience.
Organisation.
Control.

That is why every live show is approached in the same way: prepare everything that can be prepared, check everything that can be checked, and leave only energy, music and emotion on stage.
