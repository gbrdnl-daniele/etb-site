PRIVACY POLICY

The trust of everyone who contacts ETB is important to us. For this reason, we use the information provided to us exclusively to manage booking requests and contact organisers interested in our concerts.

Through this form, we collect only the information necessary to assess a performance request, prepare an initial indicative estimate whenever possible and follow up the enquiry received.

Our aim is not to create a database of customers or potential customers, but to obtain the essential information needed to properly organise a possible event.

01 Who manages the information

The information submitted through this website is managed by ETB – Eros Ramazzotti Tribute Band, through the band members presented on the “The Band” page, exclusively for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

For any request concerning the processing of personal information, you may contact us at: privacy@etberostribute.it

02 What information we collect

The form may request the contact person's name, email address, telephone or WhatsApp number, the date and location of the event, the type of venue, the requested lineup, the availability of sound and lighting services and any notes useful for organising the event.

The form is not designed to collect special categories of personal data. We therefore ask you not to include unnecessary information or sensitive personal data in the notes.

03 Why we use this information

The information received is used exclusively to assess the request, verify the band's availability, prepare an initial indicative estimate for the live performance whenever possible, contact the person making the request and manage any subsequent communications relating to the same event.

We do not request separate consent for the management of a booking request, because the user voluntarily provides their information in order to be contacted and receive a response to their enquiry.

04 Future ETB communications

Only if the user selects the relevant optional checkbox may ETB use their email address to occasionally send updates about the band, new dates, availability and future initiatives.

Consent to receive these communications is optional, does not affect the submission of the booking request and may be withdrawn at any time.

05 Data retention

The website does not maintain a dedicated database of booking requests. Communications are managed through the email account used by ETB for booking purposes.

The information is retained only for the time necessary to manage the request and any communications connected with the event. Once this process has been completed, information that is no longer required is deleted or is no longer used.

If the request results in an agreement or contractual relationship, the necessary information may be retained for the period required by administrative or tax obligations, or for the protection of the rights of the parties involved.

06 Who the information may be shared with

The information may only be accessed by ETB members involved in managing the request.

It may also be processed by technical service providers necessary for the operation of the website, email services, security and anti-spam systems, and any services used to send communications.

The information is not publicly disclosed, sold or transferred to third parties for commercial purposes unrelated to the request received.

07 Your rights

The user may request information about the personal data concerning them, obtain its correction or updating, request its deletion where provided for by law, restrict its processing or withdraw any consent previously given for future communications.

Requests may be sent to: privacy@etberostribute.it

08 Supervisory Authority

If you believe that the processing of your personal information does not comply with applicable law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority, in accordance with the procedures established by law.

09 Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated if new website features are introduced, the service providers used change or the procedures for managing requests and future communications are modified.
