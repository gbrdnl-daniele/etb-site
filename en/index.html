THE PROJECT

Behind every ETB performance lies far more than the audience can see.

01

NOT ALL TRIBUTE BANDS BEGIN THE SAME WAY.

Some simply choose to perform the songs.

Some focus on looking like the original artist.

ETB was born from a different idea.

To bring back the emotion, atmosphere and musical richness of Eros Ramazzotti’s greatest live performances, with the utmost respect for the work behind every one of his shows.

02

IT IS ABOUT FAR MORE THAN THE SONGS.

Everyone knows the songs.

The real challenge is recreating what happens when those songs come alive on stage.

The dynamics.
The arrangements.
The harmonies.
The nuances.
The silences.
The energy.

Every element helps create an experience that goes far beyond simply performing the music.

03

BEHIND EVERY LIVE SHOW LIES FAR MORE THAN THE AUDIENCE CAN SEE.

Every ETB performance is the result of work that begins long before the first note is played.

Arrangement study.
Sound design.
Programming.
Equipment checks.
Connection testing.
Technical planning.

Every detail is prepared to minimise the unexpected and allow the band to focus entirely on what truly matters: the music.

04

RELIABILITY DOES NOT HAPPEN BY CHANCE.

A successful show is never simply a matter of luck.

It comes from preparation.
From experience.
From organisation.
From attention to detail.

That is why every performance is approached in the same way: prepare everything that can be prepared, check everything that can be checked and leave nothing on stage but energy, music and emotion.

05

OUR PURPOSE

It is not to prove how talented we are.

It is to give the audience an engaging and authentic live experience.

When the technical work becomes invisible and only the emotion of the music remains, everything prepared before the show has fulfilled its purpose.

PRIVACY NOTICE

Your trust is important to ETB. For this reason, we use the information you provide exclusively to manage booking enquiries and to respond to organisers interested in our live performances.

Through this form we collect only the information necessary to assess your booking enquiry, prepare an initial estimate whenever possible and respond to your request.

Our objective is not to build a customer database, but simply to obtain the information required to organise a potential event correctly and efficiently.

1. Who is responsible for your information

The information submitted through this website is managed by ETB – Eros Ramazzotti Tribute Band, through the band members presented on the "The Band" page, solely for the purposes described in this Privacy Notice.

If you have any questions regarding the processing of your personal information, you may contact us at: privacy@etberostribute.it

2. What information we collect

The booking form may request your name, email address, telephone or WhatsApp number, event date, event location, venue type, preferred line-up, technical requirements and any additional information useful for organising the performance.

This form is not intended to collect special categories of personal data. We therefore ask you not to include unnecessary personal information or sensitive data in the notes field.

3. Why we use this information

The information provided is used exclusively to evaluate your booking enquiry, verify the band's availability, prepare an initial estimate whenever possible, contact you regarding your request and manage any subsequent communication relating to the same event.

We do not request specific consent for the processing of information necessary to manage your booking enquiry, as you voluntarily provide these details in order to receive a response from ETB.

4. ETB updates

Only if you choose to tick the optional consent box may ETB use your email address to send occasional updates about the band, upcoming live dates, availability and future projects.

Your consent for these communications is entirely optional, does not affect your booking enquiry and may be withdrawn at any time.

5. How long we keep your information

This website does not maintain a dedicated booking database. Booking enquiries are managed through ETB's official booking email account.

Your information is retained only for as long as necessary to manage your enquiry and any related communication. Once it is no longer required, it is deleted or no longer used.

If your enquiry results in an agreement or contractual relationship, the information required may be retained for the period necessary to comply with applicable legal, accounting or tax obligations, or to protect the rights of the parties involved.

6. Who may have access to your information

Your information may be accessed only by ETB members involved in managing your booking enquiry.

It may also be processed by technical service providers necessary for the operation of the website, email services, security systems, anti-spam protection and any services used to deliver communications.

Your information will never be sold, disclosed or transferred to third parties for marketing purposes unrelated to your enquiry.

7. Your rights

You have the right to request access to your personal information, request its correction or updating, ask for its deletion where permitted by law, restrict its processing or withdraw any consent previously given for future communications.

Requests may be sent to: privacy@etberostribute.it

8. Supervisory Authority

If you believe that your personal information has not been processed in accordance with applicable data protection laws, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority.

9. Updates to this Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice may be updated whenever new website features are introduced, service providers change or the way booking enquiries and future communications are managed is modified.
